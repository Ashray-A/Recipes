Pani Puri

How to make pani puri

Pani Puri is a popular street food snack in India. This dish consists of small crispy shells filled with spiced mashed potatoes and served with a tangy tamarind water. It is a perfect snack for a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • Puris - 20 pieces
  • Potatoes - 2 medium-sized
  • Onion - 1 small-sized
  • Green chillies - 3
  • Coriander leaves - a small bunch
  • Tamarind - a small lemon-sized ball
  • Cumin seeds - 1 teaspoon
  • Chaat masala - 1 teaspoon
  • Black salt - 1/2 teaspoon
  • Salt - as per taste
  • Water - 2 cups

Steps

  1. Boil the potatoes till they are soft and tender. Peel them and mash them in a bowl.
  2. Add finely chopped onions, green chillies and coriander leaves to the mashed potatoes. Mix well and add salt as per taste.
  3. In a separate bowl, soak the tamarind in 2 cups of water for an hour. After an hour, strain the tamarind water and add chaat masala, cumin seeds, black salt and salt as per taste. Mix all the ingredients thoroughly.
  4. Take the puris and make a small hole on the top of each one. Fill them with the potato mixture using a spoon.
  5. Place the filled puris on a platter and serve with the tamarind water on the side.
